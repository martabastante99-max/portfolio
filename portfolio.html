Personal Investment Portfolio

Active management of a diversified portfolio across individual stocks, sector ETFs, and alternative assets — with continuous tracking of exposure, concentration risk, and return per position.

Approach

I manage my own investment portfolio as a live exercise in applied market risk. Every decision goes through the same lens I apply to my analytical projects: what is the exposure, how concentrated is this position, and what does the risk/return profile look like relative to the rest of the portfolio?

Asset classes

  • Individual stocks — selected based on fundamental screening (valuation, growth, profitability) cross-referenced with alternative signals from the Silverway tracker
  • Sector ETFs — cybersecurity, global tech markets
  • Alternative assets — diversification beyond equities

Risk framework

Each position is tracked for weight in portfolio, unrealized P&L, and contribution to overall sector concentration. I apply a discipline of reviewing and rebalancing when any single position or sector exceeds a defined threshold — the same logic used in institutional risk management, applied at a personal scale.

Connection to Silverway

The Silverway Signal Tracker feeds directly into portfolio decisions. When a company scores high on both valuation fundamentals and alternative momentum signals, it becomes a candidate for deeper research. This closed loop between automated signal generation and manual investment decision-making is the core of how I work.
